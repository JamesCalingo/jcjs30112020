By now, I've discussed the idea of mobile compatability with this project a few times - mainly for the projects that, unfortunately, aren't mobile compatable.

Today's project, however, is actually the reverse of that - it's meant for mobile first. It's a compass and speedometer, so unless you're using your computer in some form of mobile transportation device, it's not really going to work.

This is the final project that was supposed to require a server, but I was able to get it to work without one. I believe that this is due to security stuff (like https). Also, for Mac users, this tutorial actually used XCode to simulate motion. However, since my computer has limited space and I have had little reason to use xCode in the past, I didn't use it.

Maybe I should have, because getting this thing to work took A WHOLE LOTTA WORK.

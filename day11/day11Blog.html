DAY 11: Video Player

View the finished product here

It's a video player! Play and pause the video. You can also adjust the volume or playback speed, plus there's buttons to skip ahead/back.

You may be wondering what's so special about this video player. For that, I'd like you to compare the video player in the app to a simple video HTML tag (which is something I had no idea existed before today). If the video doesn't seem to work, right click on it and select "Show Controls" and it SHOULD work.

You'll note how the control areas (play/pause, volume control, progress bar, etc) are VERY different (and also the lack of a playback speed adjuster on this page's video). That's basically what today's app is.

This one was a DOOZY to do. There's a LOT going on, and the JS for this, while fairly straightforward, looks kinda messy at times just due to how everything's done.

I also added a little feature to the player: A little thing that shows you the value you change the sliders to. I did this because I wanted to know what I changed values to so that I could adjust based on that (mainly the playback speed). The good news is that it changes consistently based on what you adjust the video to; the less good news is that it's persistent - once the change is made, the value stays there until it's changed again (which doesn't look clean IMO). I could add a timeOut function to remove the value, but I'm not sure if it's worth it right now.

As someone who (used to) do a lot of video stuff, I may come back to this if I ever wanted to do my own video hosting or something.
